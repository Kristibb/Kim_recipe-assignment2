Lovely Brownies

I am a very sweet tooth, so that why first recipe which came to my mind was a brownies. My mother is a baker, and I still remember how my mom always cooked this special dessert for me when I did not have any mood or if I had a bad day at school. She knew that brownies is the only thing which can make me feel better and make me smile. I really miss those days when I could just come back home where my mother cooked for me a home made food and for a dessert will always be "Lovely Brownies"
